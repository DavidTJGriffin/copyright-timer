Copyright Timer

Welcome!

Welcome to Copyright Timer, a place to check whether works of art are in the public domain!

This site is simple: search for a book, character, or song that you want to know about. If it's in the public domain, this site will tell you. If not, it will tell you when it will be based on the laws of the United States.

Remember: for a single or multiple author, the copyright expires 70 years after the death of the author. For works made for hire, the copyright expires 90 years after it was published.

If it's not public domain yet, you have the option to add the expiration date to your Google Calendar!

This site is powered by the good folks over at Wikidata, who volunteer to keep their data current and accurate. If you find any errors, feel free to let them or us know!

Thank you for visiting our page!
